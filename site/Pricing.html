Questions, answered

Things small business owners usually ask us.

Do I have to know anything about AI to use this?

Not a thing. You tell us about your business in plain English hours, services, what makes you special and we handle everything on the AI side. Your dashboard is designed for business owners, not engineers.

Will I have to change my website?

No. Advocate runs alongside your site without touching it. If you use WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Shopify, or anything else, it works. We install a small connector (10 minutes, zero code) and you're done.

Can I really cancel anytime?

Yes. One click in your dashboard. No retention calls, no cancellation fees, no "contact support to cancel." If you're within your first 30 days and Advocate didn't pay for itself, we'll refund what you paid.

What if my industry isn't listed?

Advocate works for just about any local small business. If you don't see your category, book a quick call and we'll confirm usually within five minutes that we can set you up the same day.

How fast will I see results?

You'll see your first AI mentions within 24 hours of setup. Most customers see a clear bump in website visits within the first week and attributed revenue by week two.

What counts as a "location"?

A physical address. If you have one shop, that's 1 location. If you have a flower shop in Austin and one in Round Rock, that's 2. Service-area businesses (like plumbers who drive to customers) still count as 1.

Can I talk to a real person before signing up?

Absolutely. Book a 15-minute call with our team and we'll walk you through the dashboard, answer your questions, and tell you honestly whether Advocate is a fit for your business.

Book a 15-minute call. We'll show you what AI is saying about your business right now, and what it could be saying.
